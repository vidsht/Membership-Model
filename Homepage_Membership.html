Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our membership program

What is the cost of the membership card?
As part of our launch initiative, this premium membership card is offered free of charge to every individual who completes the registration process.
Need a Physical Membership Card?
Your digital card is always available when you log in.
But if you'd like a physical card, we can print and prepare one for you.
Cost: GHS 100 (covers printing and admin fee)
Delivery: Postage charges are separate and depend on your location
How long does it take to receive my membership card?
After submitting your registration form, your details will undergo a verification process, including confirmation of form accuracy and validation of your linked social media account(s).
Once verified, your digital membership ID will be activated within 1–2 business days.
Can I use the membership benefits immediately after pre-booking?
Yes! Once you complete your registration, your details will be verified, typically within 1–2 business days. After successful verification, your digital membership ID will be issued and ready for immediate use at all our registered partner businesses.
Are there any age restrictions for membership?
Membership is open to all individuals of Indian origin residing in Ghana. While we welcome members of all ages, membership cards are typically issued to individuals aged 18 and above who hold valid Ghanaian residential permits.
Please note: Short-term visitors and tourists are not eligible for membership at this time.
What happens if I lose my membership card?
As our membership card is digital-first, there’s no risk of permanently losing access. If misplaced, simply log in to your account to retrieve your digital ID anytime.
If you suspect that your membership card is being used without your permission, you can log in to your account and delete it immediately for your safety.
How do businesses become partners in this program?
Businesses interested in partnering with us can apply through our website or contact our business development team. We evaluate businesses based on their relevance to the community, quality of service, and the value they can offer our members.
